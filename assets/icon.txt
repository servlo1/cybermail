PLACEHOLDER - Replace with actual 256x256 icon.png for production builds.

To create a proper icon:
1. Design a 1024x1024 PNG icon
2. Save as assets/icon.png
3. For Windows: convert to assets/icon.ico (256x256)
4. For macOS: convert to assets/icon.icns

Tools: https://www.icoconverter.com or ImageMagick:
  convert icon.png -resize 256x256 icon.ico
